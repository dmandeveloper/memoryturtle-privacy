MemoryTurtle Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-07-17  |  Last Updated: 2026-07-17

1. Overview

MemoryTurtle ("the App") is developed by Deeman Gaming ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

The short version: MemoryTurtle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. The App contains no user accounts, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising systems. Your gameplay data is stored only on your device.

2. What Data Does MemoryTurtle Collect?

MemoryTurtle itself collects no data. All gameplay progress, coin balance, unlocked decks, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your Android device using Room (SQLite). We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

What About Google Play Store?

Google Play Store may collect standard app distribution metrics (installs, uninstalls, crashes) as part of the Play Store platform. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not by MemoryTurtle. MemoryTurtle does not control or access any of this data.

No Third-Party Tracking SDKs

MemoryTurtle contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or telemetry SDKs. The app bundles only:

  • Standard Android/Kotlin libraries (AndroidX, Jetpack Compose, Room, Hilt, Coil): Core system utilities and UI frameworks with no data collection.
  • Contact email: The About screen contains an email link to dmangaming@proton.me. Tapping it opens the link in your device's web browser. No data is transmitted unless you choose to visit the page.

The "No ads. No tracking. Just pure fun." claim is fully accurate.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • User accounts or login credentials — MemoryTurtle requires no account
  • Analytics or usage data — We do not track how you play or which decks you unlock
  • Advertising or demographic data — No ads, no ad networks, no profiling
  • Crash reports or error logs — No crash reporting SDK
  • Location data — MemoryTurtle does not request or use your location
  • Device contacts, photos, or files — MemoryTurtle does not request these permissions
  • Internet access — MemoryTurtle does not request the INTERNET permission and functions fully offline

4. Data You Control

Your game progress (coins, unlocked decks, card backs, statistics, settings) is stored only on your device. You can delete it by:

  1. Using the "Reset Progress" button in Settings, or
  2. Clearing the app's data: Settings → Apps → MemoryTurtle → Storage → Clear Data, or
  3. Uninstalling the app (which will also remove all saved progress)

5. Third-Party Services

MemoryTurtle does not integrate any third-party services that collect data. The only external contact is the email address (dmangaming@proton.me) in the About screen, which is entirely optional and user-initiated.

6. Children's Privacy

MemoryTurtle does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The game is suitable for all ages and contains no user-generated content, social features, or communication tools.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included in a future app update and posted at this URL. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
